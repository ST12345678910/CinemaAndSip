How to &


1. Get Started

To 'Get Started', simply click on the 'Get Started' button! (Easy enough, right?)

2. Choose Movie Genre

Using the dropdown, select a movie genre that you are feeling for the night

3. Select Alcohol Type

Then, select an alcohol type whether it's your favorite or it's just what you have on hand

4. Suggested Pairings

You will then be given 3 suggested pairings based off of your choices!

5. Beverage Ingredients

Lastly, hover your mouse cursor (or tap) on the beverage name to get the ingredients to your drink concoction

Daily Suggested Pairings

If you are undecided on movie genres and beverages, feel free to check out our daily Suggested Pairings! (Reload page for more suggestions)
